# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and inclusive
* Provide constructive feedback
* Support others in learning

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or discrimination
* Offensive comments
* Trolling or personal attacks

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior, please contact the maintainers.
